### Creator Guide

Creating a game on Salatto is designed to be simple, transparent, and fully creator-controlled. You define the idea and economics ‚Äî the platform handles execution, fairness, and payouts.

**Step 1 ‚Äî Connect Your Wallet**

Connect your Solana wallet to access creator features.

* Click **Log in** in the top-right corner
* Approve the wallet connection

Your wallet acts as your creator identity and payout destination.

**Step 2 ‚Äî Open the Game Creator**

Navigate to the game creation interface.

* Click **Create Game**
* Choose the type of game you want to launch

This is where your lottery or instant game is configured.

**Step 3 ‚Äî Define Game Rules**

Set the core parameters of your game:

* prize type and prize amount
* ticket price
* total number of tickets or participants
* game duration and completion conditions

All rules are locked before launch and cannot be changed once the game is live.

Once published, your game becomes immediately available to players.

#### Who Can Become a Creator

Anyone who:

* passes basic platform requirements
* agrees to platform rules
* accepts transparency standards

No audience size minimum.

#### Creating a Lottery

Creator defines:

* game type
* ticket price
* total supply
* prize distribution
* creator margin

Creator does NOT control:

* randomness
* winner selection
* payouts

#### Earnings Model

Creators earn:

* a percentage of completed games
* promotional exposure

Creators do NOT earn if:

* game fails
* rules are violated

#### Creators

Creators are users who:

* create lotteries or games
* define prize structures
* attract audiences
* earn from successful games

Creators are not employees or partners of Salatto ‚Äî they are **independent game publishers**.

### How Salatto Works&#x20;

1. Creator creates a game
2. Creator defines:
   * ticket price
   * number of tickets
   * prize pool and distribution
3. Players buy tickets
4. Game logic runs using provably fair randomness
5. Winners are selected automatically
6. Funds are distributed
7. Platform takes a 5% fee

No manual interference. No hidden outcomes.
