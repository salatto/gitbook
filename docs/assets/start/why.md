# Why Salatto is different

## Why Salatto Is Different

Salatto is not another gambling dApp. It introduces a fundamentally different model for how lotteries work — one built on transparency, creator ownership, and decentralized infrastructure.

***

#### The Problem with Traditional Lotteries

Traditional lotteries are one of the largest entertainment markets in the world — larger than the entire gambling industry. Yet they remain some of the least innovative, least transparent, and most exploitative products in existence.

Here is what traditional lotteries look like today:

* **Operators keep 40–65%** of all ticket revenue as fees, overhead, and profit. Players never see this.
* **Payouts are delayed** — major jackpots are paid as 20–30 year annuities, not lump sums.
* **Forced jackpot splitting** — if multiple players win, the prize is divided automatically.
* **Tax deductions at source** — in many jurisdictions, significant taxes are withheld before you ever see your winnings. Recent Mega Millions jackpots of $2 billion resulted in winners taking home less than half. (In crypto, you handle your own taxes.)
* **Opaque mechanics** — players have no way to verify that the draw was fair.
* **No innovation** — the basic lottery model has not changed in decades.
* **No creator participation** — only licensed operators can run lotteries.

#### The Philosophy: All You Need Is a Dollar and a Dream

The core of every lottery is simple: **hope**.

People don't buy lottery tickets expecting to win every time. They buy a chance — a moment of possibility, a dream of what could be. This is not irresponsible. It is one of the oldest forms of entertainment in human history.

The difference is **how that hope is delivered**.

Traditional lotteries exploit hope — they hide the real odds, take massive cuts, delay payouts, and treat players as passive consumers with no voice and no choice.

Salatto respects hope. Every game has transparent odds, visible economics, instant payouts, and verifiable fairness. Players choose games deliberately. Creators compete for attention through quality and trust, not through monopoly.

**You cannot win if you do not play. But when you play on Salatto, you play with full information and genuine fairness.**

***

#### What is LottoFi?

**LottoFi** is the category Salatto is defining — the intersection of lottery mechanics and decentralized finance principles.

Just as DeFi transformed banking from closed institutional systems into open protocols, LottoFi transforms lotteries from operator-controlled products into an open, creator-powered marketplace.

LottoFi means:

* **Permissionless creation** — anyone can launch a lottery, not just licensed operators
* **On-chain execution** — game logic, randomness, and payouts all happen on the blockchain
* **Non-custodial** — the platform never holds your funds outside of active game contracts
* **Transparent economics** — every game's fee structure, odds, and prize split are public
* **Creator-owned economies** — creators earn directly from their games without intermediaries. Creators design, launch, and promote their own games — earning revenue from successful participation.
* **Composable** — lotteries can eventually integrate with DeFi primitives like staking, liquidity pools, and token mechanics

***

#### Why Solana?

Salatto runs on Solana for practical reasons:

* **Transaction costs < $0.0001** — buying a $0.01 lottery ticket doesn't cost $5 in gas
* **\~400ms confirmation times** — games feel instant, not sluggish
* **Native VRF support** — provably fair randomness is available on-chain without complex workarounds
* **Growing ecosystem** — large and active crypto-native user base
* **Proven at scale** — Solana handles thousands of transactions per second

***

#### Who Benefits?

**Players benefit** because they get full transparency, instant payouts, no forced jackpot splitting, verifiable fairness, and the ability to choose games based on clear rules and creator reputation.

**Creators benefit** because they can launch own games, reach a global audience, earn directly from their games, and build reputation and audience on the platform.

**The platform benefits** because it earns a small, transparent fee (5%) from successful games only — growing through volume and trust rather than exploitation.

{% hint style="info" %}
Everyone's incentives are aligned. There is no hidden house edge, no opaque operator, and no information asymmetry.
{% endhint %}
