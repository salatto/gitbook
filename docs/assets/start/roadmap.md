# Roadmap

Salatto's development follows a phased approach — starting with a focused MVP and expanding into a full-scale LottoFi ecosystem.

***

#### Phase 1 — MVP Launch

**Status: Live**

* Core lottery creation and participation
* Provably fair draws via on-chain VRF
* Wallet-based authentication
* Real-time prize pool visibility
* Automated payouts
* Creator and player dashboards
* Basic rank progression system

***

#### Phase 2 — Product & Engagement

**Status: In development**

* Enhanced onboarding and UX improvements
* Jackpot Pool — shared prize pool with monthly draws
* No-Loss Lottery — participate without risking your principal
* Scratch cards and instant games
* Creator leaderboards and social features
* Achievement and milestone system
* Leveling and progression mechanics
* Promo codes and consolation prizes
* Creator Rating (CR) and Death Game Rate (DG) metrics
* Sweepstakes format
* Affiliate program
* Guilds and community features

***

#### Phase 3 — Token & DeFi Expansion

* **SLTO token (maybe)** launch with deflationary mechanics tied to lottery activity, staking, and fees
* Mystery Box / Looting system
* Creator and influencer reward pools
* Gamified staking (Growing mechanics)
* Clan and Guild systems upgradings
* VRF-powered mini-games (Flip, Pump, Rocket, Snake, and more)
* Flip Tournaments
* API and embeddable widgets for third-party sites and streamers

***

#### Phase 4 — Multi-Chain & Scalability

* Fiat payment integrations alongside crypto
* Multi-chain expansion: TON, Aptos, Sui, Polygon, Appchain (maybe)
* Telegram WebApp
* New creator verticals: meme coin creators, subscription-based lotteries, and more

***

> This roadmap reflects Salatto's current development direction. Priorities and timelines may shift based on community feedback, market conditions, and technical progress.
