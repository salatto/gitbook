# Legal

#### Compliance & Restrictions

Salatto operates as a decentralized platform, but takes regulatory considerations seriously.

**Age requirement:** Users must be 18 years or older to participate. By connecting a wallet, you confirm that you meet the minimum age requirement in your jurisdiction.

**Restricted jurisdictions:** Salatto may not be available in all regions. Users are responsible for ensuring that participation in on-chain lotteries and games is permitted under their local laws.

#### Risk Disclosure

Participation in lotteries and games involves financial risk. Please be aware:

* You may lose the full value of your ticket purchase
* Past game outcomes do not predict future results
* Cryptocurrency values can fluctuate, affecting the real-world value of prizes
* Blockchain transactions are irreversible once confirmed
* Salatto does not provide financial advice

{% hint style="info" %}
**You cannot lose more than you spend.** There is no leverage, margin, or hidden exposure.
{% endhint %}

#### Responsible Gaming

Salatto encourages responsible participation:

* Take breaks if gaming stops being enjoyable
